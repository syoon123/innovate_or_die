Tips for calling representatives!

  1. Keep your call short and direct-- don't get carried away!
  2. Call only your state representatives.
  3. Mention that you live in the state they represent!

Guidline for phone calls:

Hello, my name is _________, I live in (my state), zipcode 00000. I (am opposed to/support) _______ and encourage the senator to (be opposed to/support) as well!
