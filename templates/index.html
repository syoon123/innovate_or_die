Civic Watch is a program designed to facilitate an increase in active participation in politics and awareness of congressional/midterm elections.

In addition to voting, ordinary citizens can and should get involved in politics and make sure their voices are heard by calling their state representatives and senators, and in this way hold legislators accountable. Civic Watch aims to make contacting them as simple and easy as possible.
